# STEM-Ranked
This is a ranking system for STEM students at I.M. Terrell based on their performance in math and science classes, computer science projects, and weighted GPA, certificates, and extracurricular activities. There will also be an overall ranking based on your scores in every other category, displaying the top STEM majors, and encouraging competition.
---

# --- Features ---
- Website that displays ranks
- Parse through a table file
- Determine rankings depending on parsed data
- Display people and rankings in order depending on the category
